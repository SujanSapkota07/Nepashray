ABOUT US


At Nepashraya, our mission is to showcase the diverse and enchanting tapestry of Nepal's seven provinces. With a keen focus on art, culture, music, biodiversity, food, and more, we bring you a unique and immersive experience that captures the essence of each province.


{ What Sets Us Apart }

 Student-Crafted Excellence:
Our team, passionate about storytelling, combines academic prowess with a creative flair to present Nepal like never before.

Seven Provinces, Infinite Stories:
Explore the richness of each province through engaging narratives, visual masterpieces, and firsthand experiences shared by our community.

Interactive Engagement:
Nepashraya is not just a platform to view stories but a space for you to contribute. Share your own tales, perspectives, and experiences to enrich our collective narrative.

Join us in this captivating journey of discovery and connection.
